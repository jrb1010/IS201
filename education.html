Education & Mission

Here's what I've learned.

In my efforts to increase my individual capital, I have found that it isn't enough to fight for oneself. Through introspection, I discovered a mantra—something to live by, to fight by, and to fight for: to relieve the suffering of the human family. I am determined in my relationships, religion, career, education, and all of my personal endeavors to bring about this purpose.
